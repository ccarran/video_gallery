Uma to shika

"Uma to Shika" is the theme song of TBS television drama No Side Manager. The single features two B-side songs: "Spirits of the Sea" (海の幽霊, Umi no Yūrei), used as the theme song of Children of the Sea, and "Deshomasho" (でしょましょ, Probably).
The title of the song (馬と鹿- "Uma to shika") stands for a word game by putting apart two kanjis of the word "baka" (馬鹿- "Stupid") with the particle "to" (と- and) to make the phrase horse and deer.

orion

The lead track orion was used as the second ending theme for the anime March Comes in Like a Lion.

Lemon

The music video for Lemon was filmed in a church and has over 700 million views, making it the most-viewed Japanese music video.
The temporary title for Lemon was "Memento." Kenshi said, "I think [the lyrics for the song] came from somewhere in my memory, but I can't really explain it well either." Even when the day before recording, he was still writing the lyrics, the last two sentences "Like one half of a fruit sliced in two, even now, you are my light" suddenly appeared in his brain and he said, "Ahh, so this song really was "Lemon". He focused on liveliness and freshness of a lemon, giving it an image quite unlike what's typically associated with death. For the song, he wanted to go for a ballad, but didn't consider that a simple ballad was very interesting, and he did not want it to be purely gloomy. As a result, the song is a hybrid of sorts, with sad words over a dancing, skipping rhythm. He made this song while on his nationwide tour, during which his grandfather passed away. Struck with grief over this event, he took longer than usual to complete this song.
The cover illustration for Lemon was made by Yonezu himself.

Loser

The single, "LOSER/Number Nine", was released on September 28, 2016 under Sony Music Entertainment's Sony Music Records label. The song itself is also made available digitally alone. It was later included in Yonezu's 4th album "BOOTLEG", which was released on November 1, 2017.
The digital downloads of the song had reached 500,000 downloads, and had reached 3rh place in the Billboard Japan Hot 100 weekly chart at peak. Meanwhile, "LOSER/Number Nine" had reached the 2nd place in the Oricon weekly chart at peak.

Kanden

Stray Sheep (stylized in all caps) is the fifth album by Kenshi Yonezu, released on August 5, 2020. The name of the song and album was inspired by the New Testament. The song "Kanden" has been used as the theme song of the comedy TV series MIU404. The album sold over 879,000 copies in its first week, debuting atop the Oricon Albums Chart. It was the top selling album of 2020 in Japan according to both Billboard Japan and Oricon. The letter calculated that the album had sold 1,970,930 copies in Japan by the end of 2020, summarizing CDs, vinyls, downloads, and streaming equivalents.
